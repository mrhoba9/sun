Our Story

Fresh, Healthy & Delicious

Founded in 2015, FreshSqueeze began with a simple mission: to provide the freshest, most nutritious juices using locally sourced, organic ingredients. What started as a small juice bar has grown into a beloved brand known for its commitment to quality and taste.

We believe that good health starts with what you put into your body. That's why we never use artificial additives, preservatives, or processed sugars in our products. Every bottle is packed with vitamins, minerals, and enzymes to fuel your body and delight your taste buds.
